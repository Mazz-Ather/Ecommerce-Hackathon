## Ecommerce Hackathon Website By using Next.js

 - first i made a new project using `npx create-next-app ecommerce-hackathon`

 ## NAVBAR COMPONENTS
 - 1st compoent is top annoucemnt bar 
 - 2nd component is main Navbar which has a logo , link buttons and search bar 

 ## HOME PAGE COMPONENTS
 - 3rd component is hero section which has a carousel that shows the top products and change image after 5 second or we can say it is a auto slider and we can also change the image manually by clicking on the center bottom side of the carousel
 - 4th component is featured product section which has a grid of products and each product has a image , title , price and a button to add to cart
 - 5th component is latest product section which has a grid of products and each product has a image , title , price and hover effect on each product and also has a sale text on top left corner of the product image and a cart , heart , search icon on hover of each product
 - 6th component is Shopex offers section which has a grid of offers and each offer has a image , title , description and 3d hover effect .
 - 7th component is second hero section which has a image of sofa and a text on right side of the image and a add to cart button .
 - 8th component is pre discount section which has a grid of products in one row and each product has a image , title , price and a line through price which shows the discount price , and a shop now top on left   side of the product image
 - 9th component is discount item section which has a heading and 3 links to navigate to different carousel slides and underline , mini circle  effect on the link that is currently active .   
 - 10th component is top categories section which has a heading and a carousel of products in one row and each product has a image , title , price and a button to add to cart and also has a shadow effect on hover of each product and also has a pagination effect on the bottom of the carousel
 - 11th component is news letter section which has a image as background and a heading and a button to subscribe to the newsletter and also has a overlay effect on the image
 - 12th component is brands section which has a image of brands .
 - 13th component is blog component section which has a grid of blog posts and each blog post has a image , author , date , title , paragraph and a read more button
 - 14th component is scroll to top button section which has a button to scroll to top of the page and also has a 3d hover effect
 - 15th component is footer section  which has input field for email ,and  a grid of links , customer care links , pages links, categories links and a social media links and a copyright text 
  ## dynamic page 
  - 16th component is featured product page which has a grid of products and each product has a image , title , price and a button to add to cart and also has a shadow effect on hover of each product 
 - 17th component is about us page which has a image as background and a heading and a paragraph and a button to subscribe to the newsletter and also has a overlay effect on the image
